4-Step Repentance

God wants every one of us to be saved and blessed, but our sins and iniquities are in the way (Jeremiah 5:25, Isaiah 59:1-2, 3 John 1:2). Therefore we cannot receive Salvation and Blessing of what 'The Lord Will Provide' without solving the problem of our sins. Only repentance can solve this problem of sins. This repentance should be based on the word 'repentance' itself in the Bible (John 19:23, Nehemiah 9:2-3, Hosea 6:2) and this is 4-Step Repentance (Christ) namely. When we read and listen to the Law and realize our sins (Romans 3:20) and acknowledge that we are sinners and break off evil by 4-Step repentance, we can receive faith as the gift of God (First Timothy 6:10-11, Ephesians 2:8) and be filled with all joy and peace (Romans 15:13)

We have to forgive others first to solve the problem of our sins by 4-Step Repentance (Matthew 5:43-45, 6:14-15)

1st step - To realize sin

Men, yet without understanding, Is like the beasts that perish (Psalms 49:20)

Through the Law comes the knowledge of sin (Romans 3:20)

2nd step - The sin written down on the horns of altar

The sin of Judah is engraved on the horns of altar (Jeremiah 17:1)

We have redemption through the blood of Christ (Ephesians 1:7)

You have wearied me with your iniquities (Isaiah 43:24)

3rd step - The sin written down upon the tablet of heart

The sin of Judah is engraved on the horns of altar (Jeremiah 17:1)

We have redemption through the blood of Christ (Ephesians 1:7)

4th step - Thanksgiving, Praise

Enter His gates with thanksgiving (Psalm 100:4)

Establish in your faith and overflow with gratitude (Colossians 2:7)

Giving thanks to abound to the glory of God (2 Corinthians 4:15)
